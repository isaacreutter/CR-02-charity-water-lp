Our Mission

Charity Water is dedicated to bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. We believe that access to clean water is a basic human right, and we work to ensure every community can thrive, stay healthy, and build a better future.

Impact Story

Samuel is a young boy whose life changed when his village received clean water. Instead of spending hours each day collecting water, Samuel now attends school, learns with friends, and dreams of a bright future. He is grateful for the gift of clean water and the chance to learn.
